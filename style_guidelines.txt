Overall Look and Feel of Schelper
===============================

The overall look and feel should be minimalist and flat.
The website should be calm and gentle for the user.

Colours
========
For Schelper, the colors on display represent calm and pleasure.
Here are our color samples:

#F39B6B
#46B0E2
#FFFAEE


Iconography
===========
We will focus on using icons that have a 'flat' design.

Typography
==========
We will be using Georgia for small text and Calibri for titles text from Google Fonts.

Links
=====
Links will not be underlined and should change color when a user hovers over it.

Buttons
=======
Buttons will be designed using the chosen colors outlined above.
